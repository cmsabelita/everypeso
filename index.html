EveryPeso is committed to protecting your privacy and providing you with a secure financial tracking experience. This page provides you with essential information about our privacy policy and contact details.

Privacy Policy Overview

Your privacy is important to us. EveryPeso is designed with privacy as a core principle. Here are the key points of our privacy policy:

We do not collect any personal identifying information such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or other personally identifiable information.

Data Storage

All your financial data is stored locally on your device using SQLite database technology. This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to share or export it
  • We do not transmit your financial information to external servers
  • Your data is stored securely on your device's local storage

No Third-Party Sharing

We do not share, sell, or disclose your personal or financial information to third parties. Your data remains on your device and is not transmitted to external servers.

Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: View all your data directly within the App
  • Modification: Update, edit, or delete your accounts, transactions, and other data at any time
  • Deletion: Delete individual items or reset all App data through the App's settings
  • Export: Export your data using the App's export features
