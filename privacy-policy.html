Privacy Policy

EveryPeso ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application EveryPeso (the "App").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Last Updated: November 30, 2025
Effective Date: November 30, 2025

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We do not collect any personal identifying information such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or other personally identifiable information.

Financial Data

The App collects and stores the following financial information locally on your device:

  • Account Information: Account names, types (savings, investment, credit), and balances
  • Transaction Data: Transaction names, amounts, types (income, expense, transfer), dates, and associated categories
  • Categories: Custom transaction categories you create
  • Schedules: Recurring transaction schedules, including frequency and notification dates
  • Analytics Data: Net worth calculations, account balances, and transaction summaries

Device Information

The App may access the following device features:

  • Notifications: Push notification permissions for scheduled transaction reminders
  • Background Processing: Background fetch capabilities for scheduled transactions
  • File Access: Document picker access (if you choose to import/export your financial data backup)
  • Sharing: Device sharing capabilities (used only when you export your data backup to save it to your device or share it with other apps)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  1. Provide Core Functionality: Enable you to track your accounts, transactions, and financial activities
  2. Schedule Notifications: Send you reminders for scheduled transactions
  3. Generate Analytics: Calculate and display your net worth, account balances, and financial summaries

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

All your financial data is stored locally on your device using SQLite database technology. This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to share or export it
  • We do not transmit your financial information to external servers
  • Your data is stored securely on your device's local storage

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • Data is stored in an encrypted SQLite database on your device
  • The App uses secure storage mechanisms provided by the operating system
  • We follow industry best practices for local data storage

Data Retention

Your data is retained on your device until you:

  • Delete the App (which removes all associated data)
  • Manually delete specific accounts, transactions, or other data within the App
  • Use the App's data reset functionality

Data Sharing and Disclosure

No Third-Party Sharing

We do not share, sell, or disclose your personal or financial information to third parties. Your data remains on your device and is not transmitted to external servers.

User-Initiated Sharing

You may choose to:

  • Export your financial data as a backup file, which uses your device's native sharing capabilities to save or share the backup file
  • Import data from a previously exported backup file using document picker features

These actions are entirely under your control and are not initiated by us. The App only accesses files when you explicitly choose to import or export data.

Permissions and Device Access

The App requests the following permissions:

Notifications

  • Purpose: To send you reminders for scheduled transactions
  • Data Collected: Notification preferences and scheduled notification dates
  • Storage: Notification data is stored locally on your device

Background Processing

  • Purpose: To process scheduled transactions and send notifications even when the App is not actively open
  • Data Collected: None beyond what is necessary for scheduled transaction processing

File Access (Optional)

  • Purpose: To allow you to import or export your financial data backup files
  • Data Collected: Only data you explicitly choose to import or export. When exporting, the App creates a backup file that you can save to your device or share using your device's native sharing capabilities.

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. Since we do not collect personal identifying information, there is no risk of collecting such information from children. However, parents or guardians should be aware that the App allows users to enter financial data, and they should supervise their children's use of the App accordingly.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  1. Access: You can view all your data directly within the App
  2. Modification: You can update, edit, or delete your accounts, transactions, and other data at any time
  3. Deletion: You can delete individual items or reset all App data through the App's settings
  4. Export: You can export your data using the App's export features (if available)
  5. Notification Preferences: You can manage notification permissions through your device settings

Note: Since we do not collect personal identifying information, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete beyond the financial data you enter into the App.

Data Deletion

You can delete your data in the following ways:

  • Individual Items: Delete specific accounts, transactions, categories, or schedules within the App
  • Complete Reset: Use the App's data reset functionality to delete all data
  • App Uninstallation: Uninstalling the App will remove all associated data from your device

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted.

Expo Services

International Data Transfers

Since all data is stored locally on your device, there are no international data transfers. Your data remains on your device regardless of your location.
